I worked with Bootstrap to create a base website that would advertise an app that helps find people to study with called "studyBuddy". Gambling Dice Game
Let's roll the dice! In this modeled casino game, I practiced with JavaScript, alongside HTML and CSS, and created a gambling dice game. When clicking the "Let's Play!" button, you are prompted to choose how many times you want to roll. The program will then automatically roll your chosen amount of times and return how much money you have either earned or lost in total.
